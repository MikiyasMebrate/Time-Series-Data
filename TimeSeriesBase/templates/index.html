Our Mission

Our mission is to provide comprehensive policy analysis, guidance, and support to drive the multidimensional development endeavors of our country. We aim to play a pivotal role in shaping Ethiopia's future by preparing medium and long-term national development plans through effective coordination with sectoral ministries and relevant government agencies.
We are committed to developing robust public investment administration and management capabilities to ensure efficient appraisal, selection, and prioritization of policies, programs, and development projects. By enhancing these capacities, we strive to maximize the impact of public resources and promote sustainable and inclusive development.
A key aspect of our mission is the regular monitoring and evaluation (M&E) of development plans, policies, programs, and projects. Through rigorous M&E practices, we assess the effectiveness and efficiency of our initiatives, identify areas for improvement, and make evidence-based decisions to optimize the allocation of resources.
