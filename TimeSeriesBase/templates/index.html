Mission

Our mission is to provide comprehensive policy analysis, guidance, and support to drive the multidimensional development endeavors of our country. We aim to play a pivotal role in shaping Ethiopia's future by preparing medium and long-term national development plans through effective coordination with sectoral ministries and relevant government agencies.
